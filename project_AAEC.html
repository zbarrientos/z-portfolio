AAEC

ASOCIACIÓN DE ABOGADOS DE EMPRESA PARA CENTROAMÉRICA

Summary

The main challenge for this project was to help the AAEC develop an elegant brand that would appeal to, and help recruit, high-level legal professionals across the Americas. It was also important that the brand reflect the culture and heritage of the region because the AAEC is the only association of its kind in the region, making it the international face of the Central American in-house profession.

Visual Identity

To reflect the region’s heritage, the brand was constructed around two visual themes: the iconic chunky figures and caricatures found on Mayan temples and traditional Mesoamerican textile designs.

Messaging

To create a unique brand identity for the association the messaging was centered around the socially conscious and ethical objectives that drove the creation of the association, as opposed to simply characterizing it as a networking group like many similar organizations have done.
