HI, I'M ZULLY

I’m a hands-on designer with an analytical and creative skill-set gained from my varied experience in e-commerce, research, consulting and art. I never look at a design project in isolation, I always bring it back to the strategic level to ensure there is cohesion between my designs and the overall business objectives.

At the same time, I’m someone who puts a premium on craft. I love working through the thousand tiny iterations it takes to make something practical and simultaneously poetic. You could say I’m a balancing act between the micro and the macro.
