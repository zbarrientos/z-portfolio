BEAUTY BAKERIE

Summary

I acted as lead UI/Product designer for Beauty Bakerie's e-commerce site hosted on Shopify. In order to help increase online conversion, I conceptualized and implemented a more cohesive, mature visual brand across the site and created reusable UI components to streamline the user experience and enhance interactivity.

Collections Pages

Current collections pages on the Beauty Bakerie site follow a traditional grid layout, displaying only rows of products. To make these pages more engaging and to increase product exposure as the user browses, I decided to break up the products with smaller ads and content dispersed in a mosaic scheme. I also cleaned up the product labeling to allow the product imagery to truly shine on the page
